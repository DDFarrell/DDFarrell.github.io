What is Black Lives Matter?

Black Lives matter is movement fighting against anti-Black racism and violence. The movement began in 2013 after the murder of seventeen year old Trayvon Martin. Since then, movement has gained national recognition for its protests fighting police brutality following the deaths of Michael Brown, 18, and Eric Garner, 43.

#BlackLivesMatter vs. #AllLivesMatter

#BlackLivesMatter isn't saying every other life doesn't matter. It's simply saying that our lives matter. Saying all lives matter to counteract black lives matter is ridiculous and contradicting. It's just reaffirming that our lives matter because they're still lives.
